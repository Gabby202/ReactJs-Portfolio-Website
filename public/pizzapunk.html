How to play:

Go to the top right purple building, talk to owner by pressing [Space]. When you recieve the breadstick, go outside and fight the ninjas with [Space]. The last ninja you defeat will drop a pizza, pick it up and dilever it at the purple arrow. When delivered, the customer will give you a [Key]. Go outside and follow the arrow, using the key by pressing [Space] at the barrier in the next level. Enjoy!.
